About Gentry Motors

What Does
Gentry Mean?

"Gentry traditionally refers to people of high social standing, refinement, class, influence, and prestige. For our brand, Gentry represents a premium automotive and mobility company dedicated to delivering luxury, excellence, prestige, comfort, and exceptional customer experiences."

Gentry Motors is not a car rental company. We are a luxury mobility and automotive lifestyle brand serving executives, entrepreneurs, VIP clients, and high-net-worth individuals across the UAE. Every vehicle in our fleet is curated for those who demand nothing less than extraordinary.
